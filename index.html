Frequently Asked Questions

Are your healthcare professionals licensed? +
Yes, all our doctors, nurses, and healthcare providers are fully licensed by the Dubai Health Authority (DHA) and meet international standards of care.
How quickly can you arrive for urgent care? +
For urgent care requests, we typically arrive within 30-60 minutes depending on your location. We're available 24/7 for emergencies.
Do you accept insurance? +
We work with major insurance providers in the UAE. Please contact us with your insurance details and we'll confirm coverage.
How do I receive my lab test results? +
Lab results are delivered digitally via secure email or through our patient portal, typically within 24-48 hours.
